Signal Policy for Brain-Computer Interface Infrastructure
TL;DR — This infrastructure is not built to store or reuse your private data. It exists for simultaneous multiplayer use — decision bandwidth, perception signal, and attestation — using BCI devices as an additional source of human proof. Nothing is retained unless you explicitly enter it.

What This Is

bci.blue, bci.red, and bci.horse are endpoints of a shared signal network. BCI devices (EEG headsets, neural interfaces) connect here not to extract data from you, but to contribute signal with you — in real time, alongside other participants.

Participants contribute to a shared state through simultaneous presence. The value is in the concurrent convergence, not in any individual's stored trace.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not store neural signal data beyond the active session
  • We do not build profiles, models, or fingerprints of individual brains
  • We do not sell, share, or retain biometric data
  • We do not require accounts, logins, or identifying information to participate
  • We do not track participants across sessions unless they choose to identify

What We Do

  • Accept real-time BCI signal as input to a shared multiplayer simulation
  • Use signal for in-session decision-making, perception bandwidth, and group attestation
  • Display aggregate state — never individual traces — on the shared TUI
  • Retain only what you explicitly submit (shared snapshots, text entries)
  • Destroy session state when the session ends

Explicit Entry Exception

If you choose to save a snapshot, post a share, or submit text through the API, that content is stored in Cloudflare KV with a TTL of 365 days and is accessible via its share URL. This is the only persistence mechanism. You create it; you control whether to share the link.

BCI as Proof of Human

These devices serve as an additional signal source for proof of human presence — not as surveillance. The goal is to establish that a living, attending human is participating in a decision, not to identify which human. Neural signal is treated like a heartbeat: evidence of presence, not identity.

Infrastructure

All three domains run on Cloudflare Workers with static WASM assets. There are no backend databases, no analytics services, no third-party trackers. The KV store holds only explicitly created shares. DNS zones are managed via Cloudflare with standard TLS.
